ABOUT SPOTIFY

Spotify Music is one of the most trusted production music libraries in the world. With a wide variety of high-quality music and world-class customer service, we have clients from all over the world who use our music in everything from personal web videos to major network television broadcasts and major feature films. In addition to the music that we provide to our clients, Spotifty Music also has been at the forefront of innovations in the music industry since its inception in 2001. 

In 2013 Spotifty Music entered into an agreement with YouTube that revolutionizes how people use music and videos on the internet. Under this new agreement, all the tracks in the Spotifty Music library will be FREE for use on YouTube (personal use only).

Most importantly, we here at Spotifty Music care deeply about the music we provide and the customers who utilize our services. We've designed our website to provide the most accessible and easy-to-understand licensing process possible, and our hands-on customer support is always glad to assist whenever needed.

Whatever your music needs may be, we're here to meet them.
